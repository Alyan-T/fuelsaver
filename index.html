🍃 Fuel Efficiency Tips
  • Tire Pressure: Keep tires between 32-35 PSI. Under-inflated tires increase road resistance and destroy fuel economy.
  • The CVT Glide: In modern automatics (like Civic/Yaris), keep RPMs below 2,000. Accelerate smoothly and let the CVT belt do the work.
  • AC ECON Mode: Use the ECON button. It relaxes the AC compressor cycle, saving roughly 1-2 KM/L in city traffic.
  • Coasting: When approaching a red light (e.g., on Canal Road), lift your foot off the accelerator early. The engine cuts fuel injection almost entirely while coasting.
🛢️ Note on Octane Boosters

Octane boosters are chemical additives poured into your fuel tank to raise the RON rating of standard Super petrol. They prevent engine knocking in high-compression or turbo engines.

⚠️ The Danger of Overuse:

Most boosters use MMT (Methylcyclopentadienyl manganese tricarbonyl). Frequent use leaves an orange/brown rust-like deposit on spark plugs and oxygen sensors, eventually clogging the Catalytic Converter. Only use them as an emergency if mixing HOBC is impossible and your engine is knocking.

🏆 Best Brands in Pakistan:
  • Liqui Moly Octane Plus: The gold standard. Very reliable, but expensive.
  • STP Octane Booster: Widely available at PSO/Shell pumps. Good for mild knocking.
  • Flamingo / Getsun: Budget options. Use only if branded ones are unavailable.
